This web chat is provided by Mibbit.

By default, your World of Anime username will try to be used as your nick, but in case it is taken by someone else on the Mibbit network, you will need to pick another nick.

To change your nick, type: /nick YOUR_DESIRED_NEW_NICK

If your nick is available, and you would like to register it to prevent someone else from using it, follow the instructions here: http://wiki.mibbit.com/index.php/Create_Your_Own_Nickname
